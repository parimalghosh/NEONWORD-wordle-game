How to Play

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries. After each guess, the tiles will change color to show how close you are.

W
= letter is in the correct spot
O
= letter is in the word, wrong spot
R
= letter is not in the word

⚡ Hard Mode: revealed hints must be used in subsequent guesses.
Press keys or use the on-screen keyboard. Good luck!
